How It Works

1

Create a Lobby

One player creates a game and shares a 5-character room code — like HAWK3. Everyone joins on their own phone.

2

Play Your Match

Assign teams with drag-and-drop, start the game, and play. The app stays out of your way while you play.

3

Submit and Confirm

The host submits the final score. One player on the other team confirms — or disputes — it. Confirmed matches go on the permanent record.

No accounts

Your identity is your phone. No email, no password, no login.

1v1, 1v2, 2v2

Supports any casual format. Same lobby flow for all of them.

Tamper-evident records

Scores are verified by the opposing team, not just self-reported.
